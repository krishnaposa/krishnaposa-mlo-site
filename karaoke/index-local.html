Karaoke Split — local stems

Why split? Route vocals to Bluetooth and the instrumental to room speakers. This page does not upload or poll Azure—load stems from your disk (e.g. Demucs vocals.* + no_vocals.* in the same folder). For cloud processing, use Karaoke Split (cloud).

Load stems

Pick the folder that contains both stems (e.g. …/htdemucs/YourTrack/), or choose the two files explicitly.

Play & route audio

Typical setup: vocals → Bluetooth, band → speakers. On Chrome/Edge (desktop), pick a device for each stream. On Windows with AirPods, choose the stereo Headphones entry for vocals if both Headphones and Headset appear. Safari/iOS doesn’t support separate outputs per element.

Now Playing: —
Adjust if Bluetooth lags (positive delays vocals; negative delays band).

Lyrics

Load a .txt file into the box, edit, then save as a download. No Job ID—everything stays on your machine.
