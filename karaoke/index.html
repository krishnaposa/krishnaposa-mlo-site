Karaoke Split

Why split? Route the track’s vocals to your Bluetooth earbuds (your pitch/timing reference) and the instrumental to the room speakers so you can sing karaoke more confidently. Upload a song (mp3/wav/m4a/flac/aac); processing runs in the background—keep this page open for status updates.

Ready! Download:

    Play & route audio

    Typical setup: vocals → Bluetooth, band → speakers. On Chrome/Edge (desktop), pick a device for each stream. Safari/iOS doesn’t support separate outputs per element.

    Now Playing: —
    Adjust if Bluetooth lags (positive delays vocals; negative delays band).

    Lyrics

    Enter a Job ID to load/save lyrics for a processed track. If you just uploaded a song, the Job ID is already set.
