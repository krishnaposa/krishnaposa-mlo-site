About Krish

Clear guidance, honest options, on‑time closings.

Born and brought up in Hyderabad, India, I came to the United States in 1994 right after my Bachelors, as an international student. After earning my Master’s in Computer Science from Midwestern State University, Texas, I went on to complete MBA at the Robinson College of Business, GSU.

Alongside my professional career in software development, I’ve always stayed connected to my passions — I play cricket in the Atlanta Cricket League, and I am deeply interested in spirituality and personal finance.

Finance has also been a long-time interest of mine. I believe financial literacy can change lives, and I am especially passionate about helping others navigate important financial decisions. That passion led me to explore various business sectors/models like buying investment houses, starting a software staffing firm, selling insurance (Term and IULs), running a Chiropractic clinic and mortgage lending.

Outside of work, you'll often find me on the cricket field, playing cards with friends, reflecting on spirituality, or sharing what I’ve learned about personal finance with anyone eager to learn.

If we haven’t met yet, let’s grab coffee or lunch—on me.
