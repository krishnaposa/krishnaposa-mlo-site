About Krish

Clear guidance, honest options, on‑time closings.

I came to the U.S. in 1994, completed a Master’s in Computer Science at Midwestern State University and an MBA at Georgia State University, then spent two decades in software engineering and running a staffing firm. Today, I bring that same analytical mindset to mortgage lending—translating complex terms into clear, practical options.

Outside of work, you’ll find me on a cricket field with the Atlanta Cricket League or digging into personal finance and personal growth. If we haven’t met yet, let’s grab coffee or lunch—on me.
