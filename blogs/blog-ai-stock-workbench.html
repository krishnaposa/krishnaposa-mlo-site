How the picks are built

The universe starts with a Python screen using Finviz-style filters to emphasize large & mega cap liquidity. It then finds strong ETFs and pulls their top two holdings. A final quality pass trims noise. The resulting symbols are sent to AI, which ranks them by the strategy you pick (Long Term, LEAP options, or Swing).
