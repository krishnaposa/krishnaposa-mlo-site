How the picks are built

  • The universe starts with Finviz screen filters to emphasize large & mega cap stocks.
  • Includes strong ETFs and pulls their top two holdings.
  • A final quality pass trims noise from the list.
  • The system updates the “cache” every 6 hours automatically.
  • You can also click Refresh Universe Cache to rebuild it on demand.
  • Once pulled, the list of stocks can be sent to AI to rank them based on your selected Strategy (Long Term, LEAP options, Swing, etc.).
