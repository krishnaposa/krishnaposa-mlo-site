Why we built it

The mortgage process can feel overwhelming, especially when you’re not sure whether a Conventional, FHA, VA, or other program is the right fit. Our AI Loan Advisor combines industry rules with plain-English AI explanations so you don’t just see numbers—you understand them.

How it works

  • The tool calculates your loan-to-value (LTV) and other metrics.
  • It returns an estimated rate range plus an AI-generated explanation of the best-fit product.

Important: This is for education only, not a credit decision. Rates and eligibility depend on full underwriting.
