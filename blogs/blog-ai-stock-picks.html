How My AI Stock Picks Work

From Finviz filters to AI ranked outputs. Clear steps. Clear decisions.

Here is the simple idea. Start with a strong universe, apply smart screens, include ETF leadership, and then use AI to rank everything based on your strategy. The result is a concise ranked list that fits how you want to invest.

The pipeline

Step 1

Universe by Finviz filters

Use Finviz filters to find large and mega cap companies, then layer in basic criteria to avoid noise.

  • Market cap: Large and Mega
  • Liquidity: high average volume
  • Quality signals: stable margins, earnings consistency
  • Optional: sector or industry focus

Finviz is a third-party screener. Names and trademarks belong to their owners.

Step 2

Best ETFs and top two holdings

Identify strong ETFs in relevant sectors or factors. From each selected ETF, pull the top two holdings.

  • Find leading ETFs by performance and assets
  • Extract top two holdings per ETF
  • Add unique holdings to the candidate list
Step 3

Additional quality filter for all stocks

Apply an extra pass to surface the best names only.

  • Liquidity and spread sanity checks
  • Earnings date awareness
  • Basic momentum or trend strength
  • Outlier risk notes if any
Step 4

AI ranking by strategy

Send the final list to AI and ask it to rank strictly by the strategy you pick.

  • Long term: durability, compounding, downside resilience
  • LEAP options: catalysts, trend, IV and liquidity
  • Swing: 1 to 8 week potential and risk control

What you get

  • A ranked list of tickers with a score and one line thesis
  • Short risk notes to set expectations
  • Suggested action phrased for the selected strategy

Why this flow

Large and mega cap companies offer better liquidity and cleaner data. ETF leaders point at what capital already prefers. A second quality pass trims the list. AI then ranks the finalists through a single strategy lens so your next step is simple.
