What these mean

  • NOI: Net Operating Income (income minus operating expenses; excludes loan payments).
  • Cap Rate: NOI ÷ Purchase Price.
  • Cash-on-Cash: Annual Cash Flow ÷ Total Cash to Close.
  • DSCR: NOI (monthly) ÷ Monthly Principal & Interest.
  • 5-Yr Total Return: (Cash Flow + Principal Paid + Appreciation – Selling Costs) ÷ Total Cash to Close.
  • IRR (5-Yr): The annualized return rate based on cash flows each year and net sale in year 5.
