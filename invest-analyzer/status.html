What these results mean

Verdict – A simple pass/borderline/buy decision, based on thresholds like Cap ≥ 6%, CoC ≥ 10%, or IRR ≥ 13%.

Key Estimates – Rent, Price, Taxes, Insurance, HOA, and projected appreciation. These come from APIs (RentCast, Zillow HOA, FHFA HPI) or fallback heuristics.

Metrics – ROI calculations: • Cap Rate = NOI ÷ Price • Cash Flow = NOI – Mortgage Payment • Cash-on-Cash = Annual Cash Flow ÷ Cash Invested • IRR = Annualized return over the hold period (accounts for sale & loan payoff) • DSCR = NOI ÷ Mortgage Payment (useful for lenders)
